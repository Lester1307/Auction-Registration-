🌟 IPL 2025 MOCK AUCTION RULES 🌟

💰 TEAM PURSE: 150 CR

🔁 RETENTION ALLOWED:

  • 1st Player: 15 CR
  • 2nd Player: 12 CR

🔄 RTM CARDS: 1

🏏 PLAYER SQUAD:

  • Total Players: 25
  • Maximum Overseas Players: 8-9

🚨 IMPORTANT AUCTION RULES:

  • 🚫 No False Bids: Fair play is mandatory or face penalties!
  • 💰 Bid Fine: 10 CR Fine for price increase violations!
  • 🔢 Bid Limits: Max 2 Bids per auction round. Rejoining after 2 bids? 10 CR Fine!

📈 BID RULES:

  • 20L to 2 CR: Increase by 10L.
  • 2 CR to 10 CR: Increase by 20L.
  • 10 CR to Infinity: Increase by 50L.

🔄 TRADE RULES:

  • ⏳ Trade Window: All trades must occur within the designated trade window. No trades will be allowed after the window closes.
  • 🔁 Maximum Trades: Teams can execute up to 3 trades per auction season. This includes both incoming and outgoing players.
  • ⚖️ Fair Value Exchange: Trades must ensure fair player value. Comparable auction price, role, or skill set is required.
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Role-Based Trading: Stick to categories: Batsmen for batsmen, bowlers for bowlers. Cross-role trades require committee approval.
  • 🤝 Player & Team Consent: Trades require mutual consent from both teams and the player being traded. Forced trades are prohibited.
  • 💰 Transfer Fee: A 5% transfer fee of the player’s base price will be paid by the team acquiring the player.
  • 🚫 No Future Deals: No promises for future auctions or conditional agreements. Every trade must be a straight-up deal with no future strings attached.
  • ❌ No Favouritism: All trades will be reviewed impartially. Decisions will be based solely on fairness and merit.
  • 🚷 No Mid-Auction Trades: Trading during the live auction is prohibited. Trades must be completed either before or after the auction.
  • ✅ Trade Approval: Trades must be submitted for committee approval. They are valid only after official confirmation.
  • 🔒 Final Decision: The Auction Committee has the final say on all trades. Any unfair or non-compliant trades will be rejected to ensure fairness.

🌟 IPL 2025 REGIONAL PLAYER DRAFT 🌟

🏏 Exciting New Rule for IPL 2025
Each team MUST draft 3 domestic players from their home region! This new rule adds local flavor and strengthens the bond between teams and their fans. Let’s build the future stars from our regions!

📝 Draft Requirements:

3 Players from your home region are required!
Choose from the regional domestic circuit players.

💸 Draft Fees:

  • Category A (Top Performers): 3 CR
  • Category B (Regular Domestic Players): 1.5 CR
  • Category C (Rising Talents): 50 L

⏳ Draft Process:

3 Rounds to pick your players.
Draft in order of last season's rankings. The top-ranked team gets the first pick in the first round, and so on.

🚨 Important Notes:

  • 📈 Ensure Compliance: Teams must comply with regional draft requirements or face penalties.
  • 🔄 No Replacements: Once drafted, players cannot be replaced unless injured. Ensure all drafted players are available for the season.
  • 📝 Draft Disputes: Any disputes during the draft must be resolved by the Auction Committee. Their decision is final.
