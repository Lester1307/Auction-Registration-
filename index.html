Auction Rules and Guidelines

  • Registration: Ensure all team details and participant information are correctly filled out in the registration form.
  • Budget Limits: Each team has a total budget of 150 CR. Ensure budget allocation is within this limit.
  • Trade Rules:
    • Each team may engage in trades during the auction.
    • For multi-team deals, a 50L deduction will be applied from each participating team’s budget.
  • Bidder Details: Provide names and roles of all bidders if they are different from the team owner.
  • Strategy and Preferences: Clearly outline your team’s strategy and player preferences in the registration form.
  • Compliance: By registering, you agree to adhere to all auction rules and guidelines. Failure to comply may result in penalties or disqualification.
